About Us

The Club of Indian Diaspora (CID) is a vibrant student-led club dedicated to sharing the rich and diverse culture of India with our school community. We organise fun and educational events that include traditional music, dance, fetivals, and delicious Indian food. Our club is a space for students to connect, learn, and celebrate together, making new friends while exploring Indian heritage and traditions.

Our Vision

To create an inclusive and welcoming space where students can:

  • Learn about Indian culture and traditions
  • Celebrate diversity and promote cultural awareness
  • Build friendships and strengthen community bonds
  • Share experiences through music, dance, and food
  • Develop leadership and organisational skills

Join Us!

Whether you're interested in Indian culture or simply looking to make new friends and be part of a welcoming community, CID is the place for you. No experience necessary - just bring your enthusiasm and curiosity!
