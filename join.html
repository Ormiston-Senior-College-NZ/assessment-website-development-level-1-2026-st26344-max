Join the Club of Indian Diaspora

Ready to be a part of our vibrant community? Here's how you can get involved!

Who Can Join?

CID welcomes all students from Ormiston Senior College, regardless of background or experience. Whether you have Indian heritage, are interested in Indian culture, or simply want to make new friends and be part of an inclusive community, you are welcome here!

  • ✓ All year 11, 12, and 13 students
  • ✓ No prior knowledge of Indian culture required
  • ✓ No experience in music or dance needed
  • ✓ Open to students of all backgrounds
  • ✓ Free to join!
