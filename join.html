Join the Club of Indian Diaspora

Become a part of our vibrant community and help us celebrate Indian culture at Ormiston Senior College!

Who Can Join?

CID welcomes all students from Ormiston Senior College — you do not need to be of Indian origin to join. Whether you want to learn about Indian culture, participate in festivals, or simply make new friends, everyone is welcome.

We believe that cultural exchange enriches our entire school community, and we encourage students from all backgrounds to get involved.

  • All year 11, 12, and 13 students
  • No prior knowledge of Indian culture required
  • No experience in music or dance needed
  • Open to students of all backgrounds
  • Free to join!

Leadership Opportunities

Want to take on a more active role? CID offers exciting leadership positions for students interested in organising events and leading the club:

Club President

Lead the club, organise meetings, and represent CID at school events.

Vice President

Assist the President in managing the club and help coordinate activities and festivals.

Events Coordinator

Plan and organise cultural events, performances, and celebrations.

Social Media Manager

Promote CID activities and keep members updated on social media.

Treasurer

Manage club funds and budget for events and activities.

Cultural Ambassador

Share Indian culture and lead educational presentations.

Teacher in Charge

Supervised & Supported

Our club is guided by Ms. Shikha Arora, who supports students in organising events and activities throughout the year.

Speak to the teacher in charge or any committee member during lunchtime to find out more!
